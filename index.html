About Us

DIRTY PILLOWS band, which originated in Stockton, California, is a prime example of genuine rock music. With sounds reminiscent of 50's rock and roll, 60's blues, and 90's grunge they are an ode to rock of the past, the music that inspires them and music that they love. Dirty Pillows' debut album Turn It Down is an eclectic mix of unfiltered, unadulterated indie rock, with something for every rock enthusiast. With sounds evoking everything from the Beatles to Nirvana, Turn It Down is truly an album with a sound for almost everyone.

Dirty Pillows formed in the Summer of 2017 when singer/guitarist William Sconce, bassist Andrew Hemans, and drummer Michael Samaniego, broke into the local indie-rock music scene in California's Central Valley. Venues range from local breweries, to county fairs, and even family weddings, the band has a set for any occasion. Dirty Pillows music has also been featured on Fox 40's Studio 40 as well as ABC 10's Sac and Co. You can check out their new album Turn It Down on Band Camp, Google Play, Spotify, or at one of their upcoming events. (Link to list of performances)

Dirty Pillows hopes to expand their fan base, list of venues, and move on to bigger gigs around the nation, while still bringing pure indie-rock to the local music scene. For booking inquires or more information on Dirty Pillows please contact below.
